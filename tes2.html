About Grace Community Church

We are a vibrant community of believers committed to loving God, loving people, and making disciples

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love people, and serve the world through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Vision

To be a church where everyone can find hope, healing, and purpose through a personal relationship with Jesus Christ.
